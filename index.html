⚠️ Important Disclaimers & Limitations

Genotype-Only, Not Phenotype

This is a genotype-only demo. Detection of AMR genes does not predict:

  • Antimicrobial resistance phenotype
  • Minimum inhibitory concentration (MIC)
  • Clinical resistance or treatment outcomes
  • Gene expression or function in the organism

For clinical guidance, consult AMRFinderPlus, ResFinder, CARD, or certified diagnostic laboratories.

Limited Scope

  • Fixed signature pack: Contains only a small subset of known AMR genes. Not a comprehensive database.
  • K-mer containment only: Does not perform full alignment, functional annotation, or mutation detection.
  • No real-time updates: Signature pack is static; new AMR variants may not be detected.
  • Browser-only: Performance may vary with genome size on older devices.

Privacy

All analysis runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your genome sequences are not stored or transmitted.

Use for research and educational purposes only. Not intended for clinical diagnosis or therapeutic decision-making. Always consult qualified clinical microbiologists and follow regulatory guidelines.
